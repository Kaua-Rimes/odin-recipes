Palha Italiana

A Brazillian dessert made with condensed milk, chocolate fudge, and broken cookies.

Ingredients

  • 3 cans of sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1/4 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 large package of a plain tea cookies
    (about 15 ounces), broken into medium
    size

Steps

  1. Combine ingredients in a large pot over medium/low heat and cook
    for about 15 minutes, stirring often to avoid it from sticking on the
    bottom of the pan.
  2. Once the mixture starts thickening slightly (you should see a few bubbles
    popping and the bottom of the pan when stirring), turn off the heat and
    let it rest for about 5 minutes.
  3. Stir half of the cookies into the chocolate mixture and combine.
  4. Stir in the rest of the cookies and spread the dessert onto a buttered
    rimmed baking sheet, flattening on top with a spatula.
  5. Refrigerate for 2 hours, cur into squares and roll them into sugar.
